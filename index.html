Personal Statement

Hello, my name is Harris and I have done my undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering in 2022. During my bachelor's degree, I was appointed as a Teaching Assistant (TA) for the Transportation Planning & Engineering Course for Spring 2022 semester in which I designed a Complex Engineering Project on 4-step Transportation Planning Model. After completing my undergraduate study, I have conducted research for 1 year as a Research Assistant (RA) on “Measuring the 15-minute city in Lahore, Pakistan”. Currently, I am employed as a Transportation Engineer in a private firm named Sheher Saaz Pvt. Ltd. where I have worked on various city-level mobility plans and regional connectivity plans in Pakistan. Moving forward with my career, I hope to pursue higher education at a renowned university for a doctorate degree in Transportation Engineering to continue my passion to improve Urban Transportation Systems.

Responsibilities:
  1. Data analysis of Traffic Surveys including:
    • Manual Classified Counts at Cordon Line
    • Turning Movement Counts at Intersections
    • Household Interview Survey (HIS)
    • Public Transport User Interview Survey (Intra-city & Inter-city)
    • Origin-Destination Survey at Cordon Line
  2. Conducting Situation Analysis of Existing Transportation System to:
    • Determine peak hour volumes at cordon line on urban arterials, mode share, directional distribution, vehicle mix, Level of Service (LOS), and Average Daily Traffic (ADT)
    • Determine Intersection capacity in the base year, average delays, direction distribution, mode share, LOS and ADT
    • Determine trip type, travel pattern and travel characteristics of people by O-D survey to understand travel behavior for model calibration
    • Determine travel behavior, mode choice, travel time, assessment of public transportation service, accessibility to communal facilities, opinion on traffic condition, opinion on parking behavior through HIS for model calibration
    • Developing a baseline condition by identifying Traffic and transportation problems in terms of road infrastructure capacity issues, traffic bottlenecks, parking issues, policy and enforcement issues, traffic safety, public transportation assessment, accessibility and quality of pedestrian infrastructure
  3. Preparing Comprehensive Mobility Plan by proposing:
    • Capacity Improvement Plans of urban arterials by forecasting traffic growth for horizon year and determining LOS, addition of carriageway lanes, dualization, or Travel Demand Management techniques to improve capacity by suggesting alternate mode of sustainable travel.
    • Road Geometry Improvements including lane marking, traffic signage, addition of service lanes and footpaths
    • Proposing new roads using Trip Generation rates for future development to ensure connectivity, accessibility and complement existing road network
    • Intersection Capacity Improvements such as channelization, addition of short lanes, change in intersection or signal type, and policy level improvements such as removal of temporary encroachments near intersection.
    • Proposing Public Transportation Network with main route and paratransit service routes with location of bus terminals, bus stops, headway, capacity, population served in peak hour and required sidewalk width
    • Parking facility proposal based on suitable parking model, parking management strategy along arterial and sub-arterial roads including parking fee management and relevant signage improvements
    • Illustrating Transportation Mobility Plan proposals on maps and figures such as forecasted traffic volumes, proposed road network, road cross-sections, public transportation network and its accessibility, parking management along roads and signage improvements
  4. Ensuring Regional Connectivity of cities by proposing capacity improvement plans for inter-city corridors, and inter-city public transportation service such as BRT and MRT.
  5. Preparation of Action Plans by development phasing and PC-1 for the proposed Transportation Network Improvements
